Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · SJK Labs

We've written this in plain English on purpose. No legalese. If something isn't clear, email us at hello@doompile.co.uk and we'll explain it.

Who we are

Doom Pile is a product built by SJK Labs. We're based in the UK. You can reach us at hello@doompile.co.uk.

What data we collect

We collect as little as possible. Here's exactly what happens:

  • Your email address — if you sign up for early access or updates via our Mailchimp form. We use this to send you product updates. Nothing else. No selling, no sharing.
  • Bills and documents you upload — if you use the Bills mode. These are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis and are not stored by us or Anthropic after processing. We never see your bills — they go directly from your browser to the AI.
  • Your usage preferences — things like which modes you've used, stored locally in your browser (localStorage). This never leaves your device.
  • Payment information — if you pay for Doom Pile access. Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

What we don't collect

  • We don't use advertising trackers
  • We don't sell your data to anyone, ever
  • We don't use third-party analytics that track you across the web
  • We don't store your uploaded bills or documents

Who processes your data

When you use Doom Pile, your data may be processed by these third parties:

  • Anthropic — processes the content you send to AI features (bills, cleaning plans, etc.). Anthropic does not retain your data after processing. See Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Mailchimp (Intuit) — stores your email address if you sign up for updates. See Mailchimp's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — processes payments if you subscribe. See Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Netlify — hosts the app. May log IP addresses for security purposes. See Netlify's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — protects and accelerates the site. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • See what data we hold about you
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time (there's an unsubscribe link in every email)
  • Complain to the ICO if you think we've handled your data badly — ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, just email hello@doompile.co.uk and we'll sort it within 30 days.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember things like whether you've logged in and which modes you've used. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us. You can clear it any time by clearing your browser data.

Children

Doom Pile is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we'll delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make a major change, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@doompile.co.uk.
